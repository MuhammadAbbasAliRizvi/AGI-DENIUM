CHIP-OFF Technology

Experience the ultimate contrast in denim abrasion with our revolutionary CHIP-OFF technology. This innovative process creates a sharper distinction between highs and lows, delivering a highly desirable visual effect that sets new standards in denim craftsmanship.

Our proprietary technique enhances the natural wear patterns of denim, creating unique textures and visual depth that tell a story of quality and innovation.

About AGI Denim

Our Sustainable Process

At AGI Denim, excellence is woven into every thread. Our meticulous process combines traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation to create denim that's both beautiful and responsible.

Sustainable Spinning

Using state-of-the-art machinery powered by renewable energy to create high-quality, consistent yarn with reduced environmental impact and superior strength.

Innovative Weaving

Advanced weaving techniques and eco-friendly dyeing processes create durable, comfortable denim fabrics with significantly reduced water and chemical usage.

Precision Garmenting

Computer-guided cutting, ethical manufacturing, and sustainable finishing techniques ensure every garment meets international quality and environmental standards.

Our Sustainability Commitment

Sustainability isn't just a trend—it's our foundation. We're pioneering closed-loop systems, renewable energy integration, and circular economy principles to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing positive impact.

From waterless dyeing technologies to organic cotton sourcing and zero-waste initiatives, every decision is made with future generations in mind.
